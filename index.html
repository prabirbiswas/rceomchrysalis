About Chrysalis

The word 'Chrysalis' refers to a transitional state of a caterpillar before it turns into a beautiful butterfly. When we look at Chrysalis 2022, it embarks the beginning for some of us and the transition to a mature beautiful person for others. Being a part of our wonderful M.C.A department for these enchanting years, now is the time for us students to give a heartfelt gratitude to our department.

This time, 'Chrysalis 2022' is not just an event, but an ultimate platform for you to show your talent and prove your mettle. We will host a 3-day event starting from 1st April to 3rd April. All events are being managed by students of M.C.A department.

Before COVID, we cribbed about our mundane college routines. The realization of not being able to work with our peers on college projects, our canteen hangouts - everything hit hard and fast and we started to long for these experiences. Finally, after years of battle with COVID we have emerged victorious and now have been given the privilege to conduct CHRYSALIS 2022.

No matter where you are, be a part of CHRYSALIS 2022, be a part of 'the ultimate transition'.
